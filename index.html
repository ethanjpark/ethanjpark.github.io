About Me
I am a native Korean but have lived in the Midwest since childhood and hold US citizenship. I received my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Northwestern in 2018, and am scheduled to graduate with a Master of Science in Robotics from Northwestern in December of 2019.

I am interested in the applications of intelligence - be that AI, machine learning, or something else entirely - to robotics, especially underwater robots as of late. Ultimately, I aim to use my skills and work in robotics to help us better understand our world.
